Swedish Space Innovation Ecosystem

  • Opportunities: Nationwide adoption across sectors (e.g., forestry, mining, finance and insurrance) via EU scale; Kiruna spaceport, ESA-BICs Sweden
  • Resistance: High-risk aversion, Strong focus on Fintech and E-commerce vs low traction in Deep Tech and Space Tech tracks; Abundant pre-seed/angel funding, grants (Almi/Vinnova) create dependency.
      Easy access to the US market creates strategic weaknesses (over-reliance on this market for growth), removes incentives to expand internationally to other EU countries (beyond the UK and Germany);
      Lack of organization's cultural diversity shades opportunities beyond EU & US (especially emerging and developing economies)
  • Solutions: Encourage cross-sector collaboration, locally and internationally. Incentivize risk-taking, leverage CASSINI EU-wide support. Diversify funding sources
